<Section icon={Target} title="Gameplay Mechanics and the CRTV">

The core of the Silent Hill Townfall experience revolves around the CRTV—a portable pocket television that acts as a modern evolution of the classic franchise radio. Instead of purely auditory warnings, the CRTV provides visual feedback through static and signal interference, allowing players to peek around corners and decipher the town's history.

| Feature | Functionality | Survival Benefit |

| **Peek Mechanic** | Allows safe corner observation | Reduces ambush risks |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Survival Strategies">

Survival in St. Amelia is rarely about brute force. Combat in Silent Hill Townfall is intentionally clunky and high-stakes, forcing players to weigh the cost of every bullet and swing. Stealth and evasion are often the most reliable paths to survival, especially when facing entities that cannot be easily incapacitated.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess the Threat">
    Use the CRTV to identify the creature's pathing before entering a room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose Your Tactic">
    If the enemy is stationary, attempt to sneak past. If they block a critical path, prepare for a defensive encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute and Escape">
    Use melee weapons for quick, desperate strikes and firearms only when escape routes are completely cut off.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Stealth Advantage">
Breaking line of sight is your greatest weapon. Use the environment to loop around enemies rather than engaging them head-on.
</Callout>
