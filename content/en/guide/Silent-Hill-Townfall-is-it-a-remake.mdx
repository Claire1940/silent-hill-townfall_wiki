<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics: CRTV, Stealth, and Combat">
Silent Hill: Townfall is a **first-person psychological horror game** that blends exploration, evasion, combat, and narrative-driven puzzles. A core gameplay mechanic is the **CRTV**, a portable television that acts as a modern reimagining of the classic Silent Hill radio. The CRTV allows Simon to tune into unstable analog signals, detecting nearby threats, uncovering story fragments, and deciphering mysterious clues.
