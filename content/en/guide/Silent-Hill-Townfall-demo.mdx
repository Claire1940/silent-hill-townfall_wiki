Silent Hill Townfall is a first-person psychological horror game, emphasizing exploration, stealth, combat, and narrative-driven puzzles. The game's core mechanic revolves around the CRTV, a portable television that acts as an evolution of the classic Silent Hill radio. Players will use the CRTV to detect threats, uncover story fragments, and solve environmental puzzles.
