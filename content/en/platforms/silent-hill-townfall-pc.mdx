<Section icon={Target} title="Unique Gameplay Mechanics and First-Person Immersion">

### The CRTV: A New Take on Classic Tools

A key innovation in *Silent Hill Townfall PC* is the CRTV, a pocket television that serves as a modern reinterpretation of the series' iconic radio. This analog device is not just a narrative tool but also a functional gameplay mechanic.

| Feature         | Description                                                          | Classic Radio Equivalent |
| :-------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------- |

| **Enemy Radar** | Use the CRTV to detect enemy locations, offering tactical advantages. | Audio cues of enemies    |

### Combat and Evasion

*Silent Hill Townfall* provides players with choices in how they approach encounters. While familiar weapons like planks of wood, pipes, and pistols are available, the game emphasizes sneaking, hiding, and evading as equally viable survival techniques.

| Playstyle | Description                                                               | Advantages                        | Disadvantages                         |
| :-------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :-------------------------------- | :------------------------------------ |
| **Combat** | Directly confront enemies using available weapons.                        | Direct resolution, resource drain | High risk, limited ammo               |
| **Evasion** | Utilize stealth, hiding spots, and the CRTV to avoid confrontations.      | Resource conservation, safer      | Slower progression, missed opportunities |
| **Hybrid** | Combine stealth and combat based on the situation and available resources. | Adaptable, dynamic                | Requires quick decision-making        |

The "peak mechanic" further enhances first-person control, allowing players to cautiously assess situations by peeking around corners or over walls before committing to an action.
