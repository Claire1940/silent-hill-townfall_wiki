<Section icon={Shield} title="Gameplay Adaptation: CRTV and Combat in VR">
The unique gameplay mechanics of *Silent Hill: Townfall* offer intriguing possibilities for VR adaptation. The CRTV, functioning as an evolution of the classic *Silent Hill* radio, would be central to a VR experience. Players could physically hold and manipulate the CRTV, tuning into signals to detect enemies or uncover story fragments. This interactive element would deepen the sense of vulnerability and isolation.

Combat, which involves both melee weapons and firearms, would also need careful consideration for VR. The game emphasizes tactical decisions—when to fight, hide, or evade. In VR, this could translate to more visceral melee encounters and tense, precise aiming with firearms, making each decision to engage or flee even more impactful.
