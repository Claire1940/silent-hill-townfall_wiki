<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Weapon Upgrades and Maintenance">

The 2026 survival horror meta introduces a deep maintenance system for **silent hill townfall guns**. Since the environment is damp and "waking up in the water" is a recurring theme for Simon, your weapons will rust and degrade over time. A degraded gun has a higher chance of misfiring, which can be fatal during a chase sequence.

Upgrading your weapons isn't just about adding more damage. It's about tailoring the tool to your playstyle. Do you prefer a faster draw to react to jumpscares, or do you want a larger magazine so you don't have to reload while being judged by the town's inhabitants?

<Callout type="tip" title="Upgrade Priority">
Focus on **Reload Speed** first. Most deaths in Silent Hill: Townfall occur during the lengthy, panicked reload animations when Simon's hands are shaking.
</Callout>

The "Heat" mechanic is your best friend here. By scanning the environment, you can sometimes see the faint metallic glint of a discarded magazine or a box of shells through walls.
